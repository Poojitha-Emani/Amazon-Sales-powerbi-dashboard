## Amazon Sales Power BI Dashboard

### Problem Statement
Analyze Amazon sales data to understand performance across categories, regions, cities, and shipping methods.

### Objective
To identify sales trends, profit patterns, and business insights for decision making.

### Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel / CSV Dataset

### Key Insights
- Technology category generates highest profit
- APAC region contributes major sales
- Standard Class shipping is most used
- Top cities drive major revenue

### Business Impact
Helps optimize product strategy, regional focus, and shipping efficiency.
